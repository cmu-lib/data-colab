Is dataCoLAB for me?

Got data? Got data skills?

There are two primary tracks for dataCoLAB participation. You might have an existing dataset and want help to analyze, organize, or visualize it. Or you might have great data science skills and want to gain experience working on interesting real-world problems. Click on either option to learn more.
