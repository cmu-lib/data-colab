Partnerships

Western Pennsylvania Regional Data Center The Western Pennsylvania Regional Data Center (WPRDC) offers unique opportunities for interested students and researchers to use existing data sets and collaborate on research and data analysis projects. The dataCoLAB team is familiar with the WPRDCâ€™s collection and goals, and can support participants in connecting with WPRDC and in working with their collection.

Carnegie Mellon University Statistics & Data Science DataCoLAB supported students in the statistics department in project to explore data captured around grants awarded to CMU. DataCoLAB provided the data and helped form research questions. The students took it from there, developing an interactive dashboard that allows users to explore the data about past grants using various criteria, identify trends, and find collaborators.
